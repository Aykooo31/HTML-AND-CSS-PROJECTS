★ Lechona, Colombia

This unique Colombian delicacy can be found in our restaurant.

Ingredients;

Lechona is a traditional dish consisting of a roasted pig stuffed with onions, peas, fresh herbs, and various spices.
The pig is roasted for a long time until the meat is tender and succulent.

☀ Pizza Napoletana, Italy

As one of Italy's most popular foods worldwide, pizza Napoletana is officially served in our restaurant!

Ingredients;

Our Neapolitan pizza topped with a tomato-based sauce flavored with garlic and oregano,
and margherita, which is topped with tomatoes, mozzarella, and fresh basil leaves

Warning!; A good pizza Napoletana doesn't need any additions other than the designated toppings!

🏆 Picanha, Brazil

Our restaurant's award-winning meal!!

Ingredients

Picanha is situated on the back side of the animal, above the butt, where it sits on a fat cap.(In the US, it's called sirloin cap)

🔥 Cag Kebab, Turkey

From hometown of our chef!

Ingredients

It is made with lamb that is marinated with onions, salt, and pepper for about 12 hours,
and the meat is then placed on a big horizontal skewer and cooked over a wood fire.

⚡ Phanaeng Curry, Thailand

A great dish from Asian cuisine that entered our menu!

Ingredients

It consists of meat that is stewed with coconut milk, panang curry paste, makrut lime leaves, fish sauce, and palm sugar.

The meat used in phanaeng curry is usually beef, chicken, duck, or pork, and the dish traditionally does not include any vegetables.
